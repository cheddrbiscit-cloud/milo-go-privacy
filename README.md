# Milo Go - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 30, 2026**

## Overview
Milo Go is a mileage tracking app designed with your privacy as a top priority. This policy explains what data Milo Go accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

## Data Collection
Milo Go collects the following data:
- **Location Data**: Used to track trip routes and distances while driving. Location is accessed only when you start a trip or enable automatic tracking.
- **Photos** (optional): Used only if you attach odometer photos to readings.

## iCloud Sync
Milo Go uses Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) to sync your trip data across your Apple devices. This data is encrypted in transit and stored in your personal iCloud account. iCloud sync is managed by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy policy. Milo Go does not operate any third-party servers. You can disable iCloud sync for Milo Go in your device's iCloud settings.

## Apple Services
Milo Go uses Apple's MapKit services for address lookup and reverse geocoding. When resolving addresses, your location may be sent to Apple's servers in accordance with Apple's privacy policy.

## Data Storage & Security
All trip data, routes, and settings are stored on your device with iOS Data Protection encryption. Data synced via iCloud is encrypted in transit and at rest in your iCloud account. Milo Go offers optional user-initiated backup and export functionality. Data is only transferred when you explicitly choose to back up or export your records.

## Third-Party Services
Milo Go does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## On-Device AI
Milo Go uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models to suggest trip classifications. This processing happens entirely on your device. No trip data is sent to any AI service or external server for classification.

## Deleting Your Data
You can delete any individual trip or reading at any time from within the app. Uninstalling Milo Go will remove all locally stored data from your device. To remove iCloud data, manage your iCloud storage in Settings.

## Children's Privacy
Milo Go is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
